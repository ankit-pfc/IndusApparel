# Post-Purchase Experiment Ideas

Comprehensive list of A/B tests and experiments for the D2C denim post-purchase experience, from order confirmation through repeat purchase.

## Order Confirmation Page Experiments

### Layout & Content

| Test | Hypothesis |
|------|------------|
| Cross-sell placement | "Complete the look" above vs. below order summary |
| Cross-sell product count | 2 vs. 4 vs. 6 product recommendations |
| Referral prompt prominence | Inline vs. banner vs. popup on confirmation page |
| Account creation CTA | "Create account" vs. "Save details for faster checkout" copy |
| Delivery date prominence | Large date display vs. inline text |
| Social sharing buttons | Instagram/WhatsApp share card vs. no social prompts |
| Order anxiety reducer | "Great choice" messaging vs. product story vs. no messaging |

### Post-Confirmation Actions

| Test | Hypothesis |
|------|------------|
| Redirect behavior | Confirmation page vs. auto-redirect to account/tracking |
| Next-purchase discount | Show "10% off next order" on confirmation vs. deliver via email later |
| Care guide preview | Show denim care teaser on confirmation page vs. only via email |
| Community invite | "Join the INDUS community on Instagram" vs. no community prompt |

## Transactional Communication Experiments

### Channel & Timing

| Test | Hypothesis |
|------|------------|
| Primary channel | WhatsApp-first vs. email-first for order updates |
| WhatsApp + email vs. WhatsApp-only | Does email add value when WhatsApp is active? |
| SMS fallback | SMS for non-WhatsApp users vs. email-only fallback |
| Delivery day message timing | Morning of delivery vs. real-time "out for delivery" alert |
| Fit check timing | Day 2 vs. Day 3 vs. Day 5 post-delivery |
| Number of update messages | 4 touchpoints vs. 6 touchpoints vs. 8 touchpoints |

### Message Content

| Test | Hypothesis |
|------|------------|
| Order confirmed tone | Transactional vs. warm/branded vs. founder-personal |
| Shipped message content | Just tracking vs. tracking + unboxing teaser |
| Delivered message CTA | "How's the fit?" vs. "Need to exchange?" vs. "Leave a review" |
| WhatsApp rich media | Product image in message vs. text-only |
| Personalization depth | Name-only vs. name + product + size in messages |
| Emoji usage | Branded messages with emojis vs. clean professional tone |

### COD-Specific

| Test | Hypothesis |
|------|------------|
| Pre-delivery payment switch | "Pay online now and save Rs 50" offer before delivery |
| COD exact change reminder | Reminder message vs. no reminder |
| Post-COD delivery follow-up | "Switch to online for faster checkout next time" vs. no nudge |

## Review Solicitation Experiments

### Timing & Channel

| Test | Hypothesis |
|------|------------|
| First ask timing | Day 7 vs. Day 10 vs. Day 14 post-delivery |
| Follow-up timing | 3 days after first ask vs. 7 days after first ask |
| Review channel | Email vs. WhatsApp vs. both for review request |
| Number of asks | 1 ask vs. 2 asks vs. 3 asks maximum |
| WhatsApp quick reply | "Rate 1-5" quick reply buttons vs. link to review page |

### Incentives & Content

| Test | Hypothesis |
|------|------------|
| Review incentive | Rs 100 off vs. Rs 50 off vs. loyalty points vs. no incentive |
| Photo review bonus | Extra Rs 50 for photo vs. no photo incentive |
| Incentive framing | "Get Rs 100 off" vs. "Help other buyers find their size" (altruistic) |
| Review prompt copy | "How are your jeans?" vs. "Write a review" vs. "Help the community" |
| Pre-filled review | Pre-fill with star rating from fit check vs. blank form |
| Review length | Simple (stars + fit) vs. detailed (stars + fit + comfort + photo) |

### Post-Review Actions

| Test | Hypothesis |
|------|------------|
| Post-review referral prompt | Show referral CTA after 5-star review vs. after any review |
| Thank-you for review | Personalized thank-you email vs. generic confirmation |
| Review showcase | "Your review is live" notification vs. no notification |
| UGC permission request | Ask to feature their photo on product page vs. auto-feature |

## Size Exchange Experiments

### Exchange Flow

| Test | Hypothesis |
|------|------------|
| Exchange portal UX | Self-service portal vs. WhatsApp-based exchange |
| Ship new before return | Ship replacement immediately vs. after receiving return |
| Exchange window | 7 days vs. 14 days vs. 30 days |
| Reverse pickup | Auto-scheduled pickup vs. customer drops off |
| Size recommendation | Show size guide during exchange vs. rely on customer choice |
| Exchange-to-store-credit | Offer store credit option alongside exchange vs. exchange only |

### Exchange Communication

| Test | Hypothesis |
|------|------------|
| Exchange nudge timing | Day 2 proactive "How's the fit?" vs. wait for customer to initiate |
| Exchange friction | 1-click exchange vs. form-based exchange |
| Exchange follow-up | NPS survey after exchange completion vs. no survey |
| Repeat exchange prevention | Better size guidance vs. offer multiple sizes to try |

## Loyalty Program Experiments

### Structure & Tiers

| Test | Hypothesis |
|------|------------|
| Tier count | 2 tiers vs. 3 tiers vs. no tiers (flat benefits) |
| Tier thresholds | Order-count-based vs. spend-based vs. combination |
| Tier names | Denim-themed (Indigo/Selvedge/Raw) vs. generic (Silver/Gold/Platinum) |
| Points system | Points-per-rupee vs. no points (tier benefits only) |
| Points expiry | 6 months vs. 12 months vs. never expire |

### Benefits & Engagement

| Test | Hypothesis |
|------|------------|
| Early access | 24h early access vs. 48h vs. 1 week for new launches |
| Birthday benefit | 15% off vs. free gift vs. handwritten note |
| Exclusive products | Loyalty-only colorways vs. no exclusive products |
| Non-monetary perks | Behind-the-scenes access vs. voting on new designs vs. none |
| Loyalty program visibility | Always-visible points balance vs. dedicated loyalty page |
| Enrollment friction | Auto-enroll all buyers vs. opt-in enrollment |

## Metrics to Track

For all experiments, measure:

| Metric | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| Repeat purchase rate | % of first-time buyers who buy again within 6 months |
| Time to second order | Median days between first and second purchase |
| NPS | Net Promoter Score at Day 14 and Day 90 |
| Review submission rate | % of delivered orders that receive a review |
| Photo review rate | % of reviews that include customer photos |
| Return rate | % of orders returned (not exchanged) |
| Exchange rate | % of orders with a size exchange |
| Exchange completion rate | % of initiated exchanges that complete |
| Referral conversion rate | % of shared referral links that result in a purchase |
| CLV (12-month) | Revenue per customer over 12 months |
| COD-to-prepaid shift | % of repeat buyers who switch from COD to prepaid |
| Support ticket volume | Tickets per 100 orders, segmented by post-purchase stage |
| Care guide engagement | QR scans, page views, time on care guide page |
| Loyalty enrollment rate | % of customers who join loyalty program |
| Loyalty tier advancement | % who move from Tier 1 to Tier 2 within 12 months |
| Repair service adoption | % of customers who use repair within 18 months |
