# Affiliate & Influencer Program for INDUS Apparels

Detailed guidance for building and managing affiliate and influencer partnerships for D2C denim in India.

## Commission Structures

### Affiliate Commission (Content Creators, Bloggers)

**Percentage of sale**:
- Standard: 10-15% of sale value
- For a Rs.3,500 average order = Rs.350-525 per sale
- Example: "Earn 12% on every sale through your link"

**Flat fee per sale**:
- Standard: Rs.300-500 per completed order
- Works for: Fashion bloggers, YouTube reviewers
- Example: "Rs.400 for every order through your link"

**Tiered commission** (motivates volume):
| Monthly Sales | Commission Rate |
|--------------|----------------|
| 1-5 sales | 10% |
| 6-15 sales | 12% |
| 16-30 sales | 15% |
| 31+ sales | 18% |

### Ambassador Commission (Style/Fitness/Campus)

| Ambassador Tier | Commission | Fixed Benefit |
|----------------|------------|---------------|
| Campus | Rs.300/sale | 1 free pair/quarter + merch |
| Style | Rs.400/sale + 15% | 2 free pairs/season + early access |
| Pro | Rs.500/sale + 20% | Full collection + event invites |

## Cookie Duration for INDUS

| Duration | Rationale |
|----------|-----------|
| **30 days** (recommended) | Jeans are a considered purchase — people compare for 1-2 weeks before buying |
| 7 days | Too short for denim — misses the research/comparison phase |
| 60 days | Fair for high-value affiliates (fashion publications, major YouTubers) |
| 90 days | Reserved for top-tier partners only |

### Last-Click vs. First-Click Attribution
- **Recommended**: First-click attribution with 30-day window
- Rationale: Fashion purchase often starts with a blog review (first click) but converts via a retargeting ad (last click). First-click rewards the affiliate who drove discovery.

## Affiliate Recruitment for Indian Denim

### Where to Find Affiliates

**Tier 1: Fashion & Lifestyle Bloggers**
- Menswear bloggers on Instagram (10K-100K followers)
- YouTube fashion channels (haul videos, style guides)
- Menswear Substack/newsletter writers
- Search for "men's fashion India blog" — reach out to top 50

**Tier 2: Fitness & Body-Positive Creators**
- Gym/fitness influencers (Athletic Taper is their fit)
- Body-positive male creators
- Fitness YouTubers who do "what I wear" content

**Tier 3: Denim Enthusiasts**
- Raw denim community members (Instagram, Reddit r/rawdenim)
- Selvedge/heritage fashion hobbyists
- Fade diary creators (document denim fading over time)

**Tier 4: Complementary Brands**
- Indian sneaker brands (SIN, Neeman's)
- Indian accessories brands (leather belts, wallets)

- Style box/subscription services

**Tier 5: Regional Micro-Influencers**
- City-specific fashion accounts (Mumbai, Delhi, Bangalore, Hyderabad)
- College fashion/lifestyle pages
- Local event/culture accounts that feature style content

## Influencer Seeding Program — Detailed

### Quarterly Seeding Cycle

**Month 1: Research & Selection**
- Identify 50-100 micro-influencers (5K-50K followers)
- Selection criteria:
  - Engagement rate > 3% (real engagement, not bought)
  - Audience 70%+ male, 70%+ India, 60%+ 22-35 age range
  - Content quality: authentic, not overly produced
  - No conflicting denim brand partnerships

**Month 2: Outreach & Shipping**
- Send DM/email with seeding offer
- "We'd love to send you a pair — take our Fit Finder Quiz to pick your fit"
- Ship selected fit + wash based on their quiz result (personalized)
- Include handwritten note + referral code card

**Month 3: Content & Conversion**
- Track who posts organically (no posting requirement)
- Engage with and repost content (with permission)
- Reach out to non-posters with gentle nudge: "How are the jeans treating you?"
- Convert top performers into Brand Ambassadors
- Analyze: post rate, engagement, referral conversions

### Seeding Metrics

| Metric | Target |
|--------|--------|
| DM response rate | 30-40% |
| Acceptance rate (want a free pair) | 80%+ |
| Organic post rate (posted without being asked) | 40-50% |
| Content quality (reusable for brand content) | 20-30% of posts |
| Referral sales from seeded influencers | 2-3 sales per influencer |
| Cost per seeded influencer | ~Rs.4,000 (product + shipping) |
| Effective cost per acquired customer | Rs.1,300-2,000 (product cost / sales generated) |

- **UPI payouts** — Indian affiliates prefer UPI over bank transfer

- **Mobile experience** — affiliate dashboard must work on mobile (many Indian creators are mobile-only)

## Fraud Prevention for Indian D2C

### Common Fraud Patterns

**Self-referral**:
- Customer creates new account with different email/phone
- Uses own referral code to get discount
- Prevention: Device fingerprinting + shipping address matching

**COD abuse**:
- Places COD order using referral code
- Referrer gets credit
- Customer refuses delivery (RTO)
- Prevention: Reward only after successful delivery + 7-day window

**Coupon site leakage**:
- Referral codes posted on GrabOn, CouponDunia, DealShare
- Random people use codes (not genuine referrals)
- Prevention: Auto-disable codes found on coupon aggregators, limit per-code redemptions

**Referral rings**:
- Groups of people refer each other in circles
- Prevention: Graph analysis on referral chains, flag circular patterns

**Fake reviews for ambassador credit**:
- Ambassadors post fake content or use engagement pods
- Prevention: Manual content review, engagement quality check (not just quantity)

### Prevention Checklist

**Technical measures:**
- [ ] Phone number OTP verification for both parties
- [ ] Device fingerprint matching (flag same device)
- [ ] Shipping address deduplication
- [ ] IP address monitoring (same IP = same household alert)
- [ ] Referral code usage limit (max 50 uses per code — prevents viral coupon spread)
- [ ] COD orders: credit only after delivery confirmation + 7 days

**Policy measures:**
- [ ] Clear terms: "Referral rewards are for genuine personal recommendations only"
- [ ] Right to revoke credits for detected abuse
- [ ] Maximum Rs.10,000 in unredeemed store credit at any time
- [ ] Annual audit of top referrers (manual review for 10+ referrals)
- [ ] Coupon site monitoring (weekly Google search for referral codes)

**Structural measures:**
- [ ] Reward in store credit, not cash (lower fraud incentive)
- [ ] Require meaningful purchase (min. Rs.2,999 order value)
- [ ] 7-day hold on credit after friend's delivery (covers return window)
- [ ] Human review trigger for >5 referrals in 7 days from same referrer
