# Upsell and Cross-Sell Experiment Ideas

Comprehensive list of A/B tests and experiments for optimizing upsell, cross-sell, and AOV across the INDUS e-commerce journey.

## PDP Cross-Sell Experiments

### "Complete the Look" Section
- Test placement: below product details vs. below reviews vs. in sidebar
- Number of recommendations: 1 vs. 2 vs. 3 products
- Image format: product flat lay vs. styled outfit vs. model wearing full look
- "Complete the look" vs. "Pairs well with" vs. "Style it with" heading
- Static recommendations vs. personalized based on browsing history
- Show price vs. show "Add and save ₹X" vs. show bundle price only
- Include accessories only vs. mix of jeans + accessories
- Show discount badge on cross-sell items vs. no badge

### Product Line Upgrade Prompts
- Upgrade card position: above Add to Cart vs. below vs. sticky footer
- Comparison format: side-by-side photos vs. feature checklist vs. fabric close-up
- Price framing: "₹500 more" vs. "Upgrade for ₹500" vs. "Premium from ₹3,999"
- Show upgrade only for Core viewers vs. show for all lines
- Auto-suggest Premium in same fit/wash vs. let customer browse
- Video fabric comparison vs. static image comparison
- Test upgrade acceptance rate with vs. without customer reviews for Premium

### Bundle Widget on PDP
- "Buy 2, save ₹999" badge on product image vs. below Add to Cart
- Bundle widget design: dropdown selector vs. card grid vs. carousel
- Pre-selected second pair (complementary wash) vs. customer chooses
- Show bundle math ("₹3,499 + ₹3,499 = ~~₹6,998~~ ₹5,999") vs. just bundle price
- Bundle with accessories only vs. bundle with another pair vs. mixed bundles
- Sticky bundle bar at bottom of page vs. inline widget

## Cart Page Experiments

### Cart Upsell Bar
- Position: above cart items vs. below cart items vs. sticky footer
- Single product suggestion vs. carousel of 3 options
- Accessory upsell vs. second pair upsell vs. care kit upsell
- "Add for ₹999" button vs. "Add and save ₹499" button
- Show product image vs. text-only upsell
- Free shipping threshold progress bar with upsell suggestion to cross it
- Test removing upsell entirely — does cart conversion improve?

### Cart Upgrade Prompt
- "Swap to Premium" one-click button vs. "View Premium" link
- Show fabric comparison inline vs. pop-up on hover
- Test upgrade prompt only when price difference is <₹1,000
- Price framing: "Just ₹500 more" vs. "Upgrade: ₹3,999 (was ₹3,499)"
- Show upgrade after 10 seconds on cart page vs. immediately
- Test upgrade prompt for each product line: Core→Premium, Premium→Heritage

### Bundle Builder in Cart
- "Add another pair and save" slide-out panel vs. inline suggestions
- Show discount tiers (2 for ₹X, 3 for ₹Y) vs. single bundle offer
- Pre-select complementary wash vs. show best sellers vs. show what's trending
- Full bundle builder tool vs. simple "add recommended pair" button
- Test whether bundle builder increases AOV without increasing cart abandonment

---

## Checkout Experiments

### Order Bump
- Gift wrapping (₹99) checkbox vs. care kit (₹299) checkbox
- Position: above payment vs. below payment summary vs. in order summary
- Pre-checked vs. unchecked (note: pre-checked may be a dark pattern)
- Single order bump vs. two options ("Gift wrap ₹99" + "Care kit ₹299")
- "Add ₹99 for premium packaging" vs. "Make it a gift for ₹99"
- Test removing order bump entirely to measure checkout conversion impact
- Add "Protect your jeans" (care kit) vs. "Make it special" (gift wrap)

### Checkout Cross-Sell
- Show mini cross-sell after address but before payment vs. no cross-sell
- "Customers who bought this also added..." format
- Limit to items under ₹500 vs. any price
- Test if any checkout cross-sell hurts conversion rate (null test important)

---

## Post-Purchase Experiments

### Order Confirmation Page
- Show 1 upsell product vs. 2 vs. 3
- Exclusive post-purchase discount (10% vs. 15% vs. no discount)
- Time-limited offer (30 min vs. 2 hours vs. 24 hours vs. no timer)
- "Add to this order" (ships together) vs. "Save for next order" (coupon)
- Personalized recommendation vs. best sellers vs. complementary products
- Full-width upsell section vs. sidebar widget vs. modal popup
- Product image only vs. product + lifestyle image
- Test post-purchase upsell with and without price shown

### Post-Purchase Email Cross-Sell
- Timing of first cross-sell email: Day 3 vs. Day 7 vs. Day 14
- Product recommendations: complementary wash vs. different fit vs. accessories
- Email format: single product hero vs. grid of 3-4 products
- Discount in email: ₹200 off vs. ₹300 off vs. free shipping vs. no discount
- "Based on your purchase" personalization vs. generic best sellers
- Subject line: product name mention vs. benefit-focused vs. curiosity
- Include care tips + soft cross-sell vs. cross-sell only
- SMS/WhatsApp cross-sell vs. email-only vs. both channels

### Review Request + Cross-Sell
- Ask for review first, then cross-sell vs. cross-sell only
- "Love your jeans? Complete the look" vs. "Based on your 5-star review..."
- Cross-sell in review confirmation page vs. in follow-up email
- Photo review incentive (₹200 credit) + cross-sell vs. just cross-sell

## Bundle Pricing Experiments

### Discount Level
- 10% bundle discount vs. 15% vs. 20%
- Percentage off ("Save 15%") vs. rupee amount ("Save ₹999")
- Free accessory with 2+ jeans vs. discount on jeans bundle
- Tiered discounts (2 for 10% off, 3 for 20% off) vs. flat discount
- Bundle discount vs. free shipping on bundles vs. both

### Bundle Composition
- Same-category bundles (2 jeans) vs. cross-category (jeans + belt)
- Customer-curated bundles ("Pick any 2") vs. pre-set bundles
- Seasonal bundles (summer collection set) vs. evergreen bundles
- Mix-and-match across lines (Core + Premium) vs. same line only
- Gift-ready bundles (with packaging) vs. standard bundles

### Bundle Presentation
- Dedicated bundle page vs. bundle widget on PDP vs. both
- Bundle landing page with all options vs. "build your bundle" interactive tool
- Show savings prominently ("Save ₹999") vs. show bundle price only
- Bundle comparison table vs. visual outfit layout
- "Most popular bundle" social proof vs. no social proof
- Limited-time bundle ("This week only") vs. always available

## Returns Page Experiments

### Exchange Upsell
- Offer upgrade during exchange flow vs. same-product exchange only
- "Upgrade to Premium for ₹500 more" during size exchange
- Store credit + ₹200 bonus vs. straight refund as default option
- "Try a different fit" suggestion vs. "Try a different wash"
- Free priority shipping on exchanges vs. standard shipping
- Show product recommendations during return flow vs. clean return experience

### Return Recovery
- Offer to exchange before return is processed vs. process return first
- Chat support during return (save the sale) vs. self-service only
- "Would a different size fix this?" sizing quiz during return
- Discount code for next purchase in return confirmation vs. no incentive
- Test if return page cross-sell increases overall return satisfaction

---

## WhatsApp/SMS Experiments

### Abandoned Cart Recovery
- Timing: 1 hour vs. 4 hours vs. 24 hours for first message
- Message format: product image vs. text-only vs. catalog card
- Discount: ₹200 off vs. free shipping vs. no incentive vs. bundle offer
- Number of follow-ups: 1 vs. 2 vs. 3 messages
- WhatsApp vs. SMS vs. both channels
- Personalized message ("Hi [name], your [product] is waiting") vs. generic
- Include upsell in recovery message vs. focus on original product only

### Post-Purchase WhatsApp
- Timing: Day 5 vs. Day 7 vs. Day 10 after delivery
- Open with care tip then cross-sell vs. direct product recommendation
- WhatsApp catalog message vs. single product with link
- "How's the fit?" conversation starter vs. direct recommendation
- Exclusive WhatsApp price vs. same price as website

---

## Personalization Experiments

### Browsing-Based
- Recommend products based on browsing history vs. best sellers
- Show recently viewed as "Still interested?" on cart page
- Personalize "Complete the look" based on viewed products vs. static pairings
- Dynamic homepage hero based on last category viewed vs. standard hero

### Purchase-Based
- Recommend different fit (if they bought slim, suggest taper) vs. same fit new wash
- Recommend same line vs. upgrade line for returning customers
- "Your size is in stock" notifications for new products vs. generic launch emails
- Loyalty-based pricing (repeat customers see exclusive offers) vs. same price for all

### Segment-Specific
- Different upsell strategy for first-time vs. returning customers
- Higher-value upsell for high-AOV customers vs. same offer for everyone
- Accessory-focused upsell for those who've bought jeans before vs. jeans-focused
- Geographic personalization (metro vs. Tier 2 pricing/offers)

---

## Timing and Frequency Experiments

### Frequency Capping
- Max 2 upsell touchpoints per session vs. 3 vs. unlimited
- Cool-down between upsell touchpoints (1 page vs. 3 pages)
- Cap post-purchase emails at 2/month vs. 4/month
- Test if reducing upsell frequency improves overall conversion and satisfaction

### Seasonal Timing
- Increase bundle offers during festive season vs. same year-round
- Gift bundle push 2 weeks before Diwali vs. 4 weeks before
- End-of-season upsell ("Last chance at this price") vs. standard upsell
- Pre-order upsell for new collection 1 week before vs. 2 weeks before launch

### Journey-Stage Timing
- Show upsell after 30 seconds on PDP vs. immediately vs. on scroll
- Cart upsell on page load vs. after 5 seconds vs. on scroll to bottom
- Post-purchase upsell: immediate on confirmation vs. 10 minute delay email
- Trigger cart upsell only when user moves toward checkout button vs. always visible
